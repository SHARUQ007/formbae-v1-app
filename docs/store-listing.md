## Full description
> FormBae pairs you with a recommended human expert personal trainer within your budget and gives you a workout plan built around your goal, schedule, and whether you train at home or the gym.
>
> - Free fitness analysis based on your goals and lifestyle
> - A recommended male or female expert trainer matched to you
> - A structured weekly workout plan with guided exercises and rest timers
> - Progress tracking, weekly check-ins, and direct messaging with your trainer
> - Reminders to keep you consistent
>
> Already started on the FormBae website? Log in with the same phone number and your plan syncs automatically — no need to repeat anything.
>
> FormBae provides fitness coaching and guidance, not medical advice. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any exercise program.
